Project

Wander[001] is an android travelling on the future earth.Send her any real-world location on discord or wechat, she will visit the future, and send back travel log, photo and GPS location. This map documented people's travel log.To start a journey, please interact with Wander first on discord channel. Your first part of the journey will show up on this map.All content is generated through AI with real-world information.
had been accepted by ACM MULTIMEDIA 2022 Artwork
